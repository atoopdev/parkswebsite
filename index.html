Welcome

Greetings and welcome to my election website for the Hood River Valley Parks and Recreation District Board. I am Keir Bryerton, and I am excited to share my vision for the future of our community's beloved parks and recreational areas.

Here, you will discover my perspectives on park issues that matter to me, such as financial management, community involvement, land use, and environmental impact.

Also, please take a moment to explore the "About Me" section, where you can learn more about my background.

I would be honored to have the opportunity to serve our community as a board member. Thank you for your interest, and I look forward to earning your trust and support!

Election Day is May 16, 2023. Ballots must be postmarked or dropped off by 8pm.
