Aquatic Center

Yes to Aquatic Center

I am 100% behind a full replacement of the aquatic center.

The infrastructure supporting the pool is too old to make further renovations a common sense approach.

At the April 2023 Parks and Rec Board meeting, 3 options for replacement were presented with price tags of 36.5 million, 40 million, and 47.8 million.

The price differences reflected how much additional space would be built to support recreational activities: Aquatic Center vs. Aquatic and Recreation Center.

Hood River Valley Parks and Recreation District needs to thoroughly vet these options with the community and investigate a diversified funding strategy.

Funding options could include:

  • Bond measure approved by voters
  • Grants
  • Support from the business community and other municipal agencies
  • Taxes levied on tourism such as short term rental tax and/or restaurant tax
  • Philanthropy
